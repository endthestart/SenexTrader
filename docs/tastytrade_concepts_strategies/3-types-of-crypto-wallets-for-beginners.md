# 3 Types of Crypto Wallets for Beginners

Source: https://www.tastylive.com/concepts-strategies/3-types-of-crypto-wallets-for-beginners

The article discusses what a crypto wallet is, the difference between public and private keys, custodial vs. non-custodial wallets, and hot vs. cold wallets. It then details three types of crypto wallets: paper wallets, hardware wallets, and software wallets (web, mobile, and desktop). The article also provides guidance on choosing the best type of crypto wallet based on user needs.
